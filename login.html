Orpheus is an academic project — a full-featured music streaming platform crafted from scratch as a final-year BCA capstone, powered by vanilla web technologies & Supabase.

Live Streaming — stream songs from a real cloud database
Studio Equalizer — 10-band EQ to sculpt your sound
Echo Analytics — visualise your listening patterns
Liked Songs & Playlists — build your personal library
Trending Charts — discover what's hot in India & worldwide
Zero Ads. Pure music, always — no interruptions
