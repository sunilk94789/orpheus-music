2. Your Data Rights Under Indian Law

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and related Indian legislation, you have the following rights as a Data Principal:

Right to Information

You are entitled to know what personal data we process, why, and how. This Policy and our in-app notices fulfil this obligation.

Right to Access

Request a summary of personal data we hold about you via Account Settings → Download My Data, or by contacting privacy@orpheusmusic.in.

Right to Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data via account settings or by contacting our Data Protection Officer.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data. Deletion may be limited where we have a legal obligation to retain data (e.g., financial records under tax law, fraud prevention). Account closure erases all profile and usage data within 30 days.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on your consent (e.g., marketing emails, personalised recommendations), you may withdraw consent at any time via Settings. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of prior processing.

Data Portability

Request your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format for transfer to another service.

Right to Grievance Redressal

Lodge a complaint with our Data Protection Officer. Unresolved complaints may be escalated to the Data Protection Board of India once constituted under the DPDPA, 2023.

Identity Verification

We may request identity verification before fulfilling data requests to protect your privacy. Authorised agents may act on your behalf with documented authorisation.

4. How We Use Your Data

We process your personal data only for the following purposes, each with a defined legal basis under the DPDPA, 2023:

  • Service Delivery — To create and manage your account, stream content, and process payments. (Contractual necessity)
  • Personalisation — To power recommendations, curated playlists, and Echo Analytics. (Consent / Legitimate interest)
  • Security & Fraud Prevention — To detect and prevent abuse, unauthorised access, and fraudulent transactions. (Legitimate interest / Legal obligation)
  • Legal Compliance — To comply with Indian law, respond to lawful authority requests, and defend legal claims. (Legal obligation)
  • Product Improvement — To fix bugs, develop features, and train recommendation models using anonymised data. (Legitimate interest)
  • Marketing — To send promotional communications about Orpheus features (opt-out available any time). (Consent)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for their independent marketing purposes.

5. Sharing Your Data

What May Be Visible to Other Users

  • Your display name and profile avatar
  • Public playlists you create
  • Content you choose to share

Your email address, date of birth, and listening history are never visible to other users.

Service Providers

We share data with vetted sub-processors who provide cloud hosting, payment processing, email delivery, and analytics — all under strict data processing agreements that prohibit independent use of your data.

Legal Disclosures

We may disclose personal data to law enforcement, government authorities, or courts when required by a valid legal order under Indian law. We will notify you where legally permitted.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, personal data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections. You will be notified of any such change.

6. Data Security

Orpheus implements industry-standard technical and organisational safeguards consistent with the IT (Reasonable Security Practices) Rules, 2011:

AES-256 Encryption at rest
TLS 1.3 in transit
Role-based access controls
Regular security audits
Password hashing (bcrypt)
OTP email verification

In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you and the relevant Indian authorities within 72 hours of becoming aware, as required by applicable law.

Protect your account:

  • Use a strong, unique password for Orpheus
  • Never share your OTP or password with anyone
  • Log out of shared devices after use
  • Enable account notifications for unusual activity

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy:

  • Account data — Retained for the duration of your account. Deleted within 30 days of account closure.
  • Usage data — Retained for up to 24 months for personalisation and analytics, then anonymised.
  • Payment records — Retained for 7 years as required under Indian financial and tax laws.
  • OTP logs — Deleted within 10 minutes of generation or on use, whichever comes first.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such data without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it promptly. Users aged 13–17 require parental/guardian consent as per the DPDPA, 2023.

If you believe a child has provided us data without consent, please contact privacy@orpheusmusic.in.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential cookies — Required for login sessions and security (cannot be disabled).
  • Preference cookies — To remember your settings (theme, equalizer presets).
  • Analytics cookies — To understand how the Service is used (anonymised; opt-out available).

You can manage non-essential cookies via your browser settings or the cookie preferences panel in the app.
