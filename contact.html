About Orpheus

ACADEMIC PROJECT · BCA 3RD YEAR · 2026

Orpheus is a personalised digital music streaming platform built as a final-year project for the Bachelor of Computer Applications programme at Goswami Ganesh Dutta Sanatan Dharma College. The platform features full song streaming from a live Supabase database, a dynamic music player, custom playlists, an equaliser, and a polished dark/light UI — all crafted from scratch.

The name Orpheus is drawn from Greek mythology — the legendary musician whose music could move even the stones. We built this as a tribute to that idea: music that moves people.
